## 🎯 Business Logic Summary

### Marquee 1 Pattern (Inverse Hierarchy):
1. **Lunch (1-4 PM)**: Prime Space books entire venue
   - Result: Sub-spaces hidden during lunch
2. **Dinner (6-10 PM)**: Sub-spaces book individually
   - Result: Prime space hidden during dinner

### Marquee 2 Pattern (Inverse Hierarchy):
1. **Lunch (1-4 PM)**: Sub-spaces book individually
   - Result: Prime space hidden during lunch
2. **Dinner (6-10 PM)**: Prime Space books entire venue
   - Result: Sub-spaces hidden during dinner
